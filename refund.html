Refund & Replacement Policy

Customer satisfaction is our top priority. Our policies are designed to ensure a secure and smooth experience.

1. Refund Policy

  • Refunds are not applicable after order confirmation
  • Product changes are allowed before confirmation
  • Refunds apply if delivery is delayed or mismatched

2. Digital Product Policy

Once a digital subscription is delivered, it is generally non-refundable.

3. Refund Conditions

  • Within warranty → Fix or Replacement first
  • If unresolved → Partial Refund based on remaining time

Example: 4 months plan → 2 months used → refund for remaining 2 months

4. Refund Processing Time

  • bKash / Nagad / Bank → 1–3 working days
  • Payment Gateway (EPS) → 3–7 working days

5. Warranty

  • All subscriptions include Full Period Warranty
  • Issues within warranty → free fix or replacement

6. Additional Charges

  • No charge if issue is from our side
  • Charges may apply if caused by customer

7. Support

Contact us via WhatsApp for any issue or assistance. Our team will respond as quickly as possible.
